Classes

Move with intention

Ashtanga Roots

Grounded in Ashtanga yoga principles, opening into creative Vinyasa flow. Steady breath, intentional sequencing, and mindful transitions. Balances tradition with freedom of movement.

Dance & Martial Art

A dynamic practice blending contemporary dance, martial arts, somatics, and breath. Movement becomes a language for exploration and embodied awareness.

Yoga Skills

One pose per session, broken down through precise preparation. Builds technique, strength, and control. A deeper understanding of advanced postures.

Dynamic Yoga

Flowing, rhythmic, and challenging. Ashtanga-based asanas with creative transitions. Breath, mobility, and continuous movement, supported by music.

Modern Dance

Technically grounded and fluid, focusing on floor work, musicality, and coordination. A supportive space for developing personal movement language.

Handstand, Mobility & Strength

Focused practice for building a stable handstand. Develops shoulder mobility, core strength, and proper alignment with emphasis on safety and control.

Movement

Open exploration of movement without fixed forms. Encourages curiosity, play, and embodied expression.

Vinyasa Yoga

Dynamic, high-energy practice with options for advanced postures. Invites challenge, exploration, and personal pacing.
