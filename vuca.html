Classes

Train with purpose

Box & Burn

A high-energy class built around boxing-based movement. Full-body workout combining bag work and HIIT, blending aerobic and anaerobic effort. Challenging yet accessible. Open to all fitness levels.

Boxing Basics

Core principles of boxing through focused drills and bag work. Covers stance, punches, footwork, and defense in a no-contact format. Emphasis on precision, control, and form.

Boxing Intermediate

Builds on foundational skills with controlled partner work. Focuses on timing, distance, and technique. A structured step toward sparring with emphasis on control and safety.

Movement Martial

Draws on martial arts to develop strength, mobility, and rapid responsiveness. Through floor work and precise opening of the body, it builds efficient and controlled movement.
