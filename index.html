Who We Are

Not a gym.
Not a spa.
A system.

De Club is a concept wellness club built for people who live between contrasting states. Pushing limits in the morning, finding stillness in the afternoon, and coming back for more.

Across three floors, we've designed a single ecosystem where boxing, precision movement, and deep recovery aren't separate activities. They're one practice.

Self-care in motion is at our core.

The Four Floors

One club.
Four worlds.

Each floor is designed for a different state of being. Together, they form a complete system for your body and mind.

What Moves Us

Built for
the everyday

We don't believe in burning out. De Club is designed so you can come every single day. Sometimes to push, sometimes to recover, always to show up for yourself.

Chaos and stillness. Power and precision. Heat and cold. We believe the tension between opposites is where real growth happens.

An entire floor dedicated to recovery isn't a luxury. It's the foundation. You can't train hard if you don't recover harder.

The people around you shape your practice. De Club attracts people who are serious about showing up, and that energy is contagious.
